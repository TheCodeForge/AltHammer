{% extends "_core_centered.html" %} {% block pagecontent %} {% filter markdown %} # Strategems All factions and Detachments may use the following Strategems. The Command Point cost of a Strategem is indicated in brackets next to its name. > **[1] Command Re-Roll** > > When: Immediately after making a Hit roll, Wound roll, Save roll, Battle-shock test, Psychic test, or when rolling to determine the number of attacks or amount of damage in a weapon profile > > Target: The friendly Unit or Model making that roll. > > Effect: Reroll that roll. For rolls of more than one die (Battle-Shock and Psychic tests), all dice must be rerolled. > **[1] Insane Bravery** > > When: Immediately after failing a Battle-Shock test > > Target: The friendly Unit that test was taken for (even though Battle-Shocked units cannot normally be affected by Strategems) > > Effect: Your Unit is treated as having passed the Battle-Shock test instead. > **[1] Overwatch** > > When: Immediately after an enemy Unit Moves, Charges, or is deployed from Reserves > > Target: A friendly Unit. > > Effect: Your Unit may perform a Shoot action, targeting the enemy Unit that just Moved. > **[1] Hit the Ground** > > When: Immediately after an enemy Unit declares a Shoot action. > > Target: A friendly +Infantry+ Unit that was declared as the target of that Shoot action. > > Effect: All Models in that Unit have a 6++ Invulnerable Save and the Benefit of Cover. This lasts until the end of the Battle Round, or until the Unit performs a Move or Charge action, whichever occurs first. {% endfilter %} {% endblock %}
