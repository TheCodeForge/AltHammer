# Leader During the [Reveal Armies and Formations](/rules/core_rules#reveal_armies_and_formations) step, attach this unit to an +Infantry+ or +Battleline+ unit. The leading unit gainst the +Leader+ keyword, and the unit to which it attached gains the +Bodyguard+ keyword. For the rest of the battle, this combined force is treated as a single unit with a starting strength of the combined model count. If either the +Leader+ or all +Bodyguard+ models are destroyed, the remaining models lose the +Leader+ or +Bodyguard+ keywords, and make their determination of starting strength based on the original uncombined unit's model count.
