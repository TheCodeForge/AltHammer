# Introduction AltHammer is a turn-based activation tabletop wargaming experience. It is intended to be played with your existing collection of wargaming models. # General Rules ## Priority In the event of inconsistency between two sources of rules, use the rule that is found in the earliest location in this list: * Unit rules card * Detachment Rules * Faction Rules * Core Rules # Definitions See [Definitions](/core_rules/definitions) # Order of Play ## Determine Points Limit Together with your opponent, agree on a points limit for the game of at least 500 points or higher. Suggested limits: * Small: 500 points * Medium: 1000 points * Large: 2000 points * Epic: 3000+ points Additionally, if both players agree to it, the Sideboard optional rule may be enabled. ## Select Faction and Detachment Select your army Faction and Detachment. You do not have to reveal your selections to your opponent at this time, but if your opponent reveals their selections to you, consider reciprocating. ## Muster Army Assemble a collection of Units in your chosen Faction, up to the agreed points limit. Requirements: * All Units must have the Faction Keyword matching your chosen Faction. All units must satisfy any additional restrictions established by your chosen Detachment. * You must include at least one +Character+ model. * You may not include more than one copy of an +Epic Hero+ unit. * You may not include more than six copies of a +Front Lines+ unit. * You may not include more than three copies of any other unit Select one +Character+ Model as your army's +Warlord+. ## Set Up Battlefield Set up a tabletop battlefield according to the agreed upon points limit: * 500-1999 points: 44" x 60" * 2000+ points: 44" x 90" Add terrain features in a mutually agreeable manner. Overall terrain layout should be either reflectively or rotationally symmetric. Ensure that the classification of all terrain features is mutually understood and agreed upon. See [Terrain](/core_rules/terrain) ## Reveal Armies Reveal your chosen Faction, Detachment, and mustered Units to the opposing player, if you have not already done so. Identify which Model is your +Warlord+. ## Sideboard Option The Sideboard Option may be enabled by mutual agreement between players during the Determine Points Limit step. If any player declines the Sideboard Option, it is not enabled. If the Sideboard Option is enabled: * During the Muster Army step, assemble a collection of Units up to 500 points *above* the agreed points limit instead. * After the Reveal Army step, Roll Off. Players alternate selecting and removing Units from their mustered army, until their remaining army is at or below the agreed points limit, beginning with the player who lost the Roll Off. * Your +Warlord+ Model must remain in your army. # Activation When Activated, a Unit may do up to two of the following actions: * Move * Advance * Magik (+Psychic+ Units only) * Shoot * Charge * Fight A unit may not Magik, Shoot, or Charge in an Activation in which it also Advances. A unit may not be Activated two turns in a row, unless it is the last remaining Unit in the army. ## Move Each Model in the Unit may move up to the number of inches indicated by its Movement characteristic. Models may use their Movement to move horizontally, or vertically up or down terrain. Models with +Fly+ may move diagonally through the air to go over terrain. ### Restrictions Units in Melee Distance of an Enemy Unit may not move through enemy Models. Models may not end their movement on another Model or its base Models may not move through Enemy Models. Models may not end a movement within Melee Distance of Enemy Models. Models may not end their movement partially up a wall or other terrain obstacle. Units must end their movement in Unit Coherency. ## Advance Increase the Unit's Move characteristic by 1d6 inches for the duration of the Activation, then follow the Move instructions above. ## Magik Only +Psychic+ Models may perform this action. Select a spell from your Detachment rules. For each +Psychic+ Model in the Unit, make a Psychic test: Roll 2d6; add 1 to the result if the Model is an +Epic Hero+, and add 1 to the result if the Model is your +Warlord+. If the result is greater than the spell's Difficulty, then the spell is successful. Resolve the effects of the spell. If the result of the Psychic test was an unmodified 2 or unmodified 12, then the Model suffers Perils of Power: The Model's unit suffers d3 Mortal Damage. (For an unmodified 12, the spell is still successful; resolve the spell's effects first.) All +Psychic+ Models know the spell Smite: > Smite (Difficulty: 6) > > Select a target. It suffers d6 Mortal Damage. The Difficulty of this spell increases by 1 for your army. ## Shoot Select a target Enemy Unit. For each Model in your Unit with line-of-sight to at least one Enemy Unit Model, select a Ranged weapon profile. Roll dice. ## Charge Select a target Enemy Unit. Roll 2d6 for Charge Movement; if the result is greater than or equal to the distance to the enemy unit (inches, rounded down), move the Unit directly towards the enemy unit until they are in Melee Distance. No Model may move a greater distance than the Charge Movement result. +Aircraft+ cannot be selected as a Charge target unless all models in the charging Unit have +Fly+. ## Fight Select a target Enemy Unit that is within Melee Distance. Choose a melee weapon profile and roll dice. +Aircraft+ cannot be selected as a Fight target unless all models in the attacking Unit have +Fly+. # two # three ## three.one
